Circle of life

Rules:

    Two players. One board.
    Click on a hex to occupy it.
    Then the other player does the same.
    One move per player.
    One cannot occupy a hex if it is near
    a structure of his own other 4 hex spaces.
    However, if a player makes a shape that
    eliminates another, the player who lost
    the shape loses as many points
    as lost shapes.
    Both player start from 22 points!
